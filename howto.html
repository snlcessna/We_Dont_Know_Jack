The object of We Don't Know Jack is to be the first to guess the correct answer. The player with the highest score will win.

This is a 2-player game.

The 'A' Key is applied to player 1. The 'L' key is applied to player 2.

When you begin the game you will need to enter your name and choose whether you'd like to play a long or short game. The short game has 5 questions. The long game has 11 questions.

You and your opponent may buzz in by pressing the 'A' key and 'L' key. The quicker you are to draw, the more likely you are to score points.

The player that buzzes in first will have his or her name backlit with an ethereal glow.

WARNING: If you guess wrong, you will lose points. Also, the game will not advance to a new round until someone gets the question correct.
